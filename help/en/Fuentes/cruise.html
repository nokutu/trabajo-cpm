Cruise information panel

In this panel you can see the detailed information about the selected cruise. In the lateral panel you can select a date, cabin and amount of people that you wish to book.

Extras

It is possible to choose among a set of extras for the cabin. Keep in mind that they will increase the cost depending on the number of passengers and days. In the speciasl case of the extra bed, it allows to have one more passenger in the cabin, but can only be used by a child younger than 16.

Multiple cabins

It is posible to book more than one cabin in the same order. To do so, you must choose the first one and then press the button "Book more cabins". Keep in mind that this will block the date field, as you can only book for a single date.

Minors

A child is considered a minor if he is younger than 16.
